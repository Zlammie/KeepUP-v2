Welcome to Willow Grove

Willow Grove is a mini master planned community designed with an artist's touch, ideal for those who value quality living and a strong sense of neighborhood. The community features Townhomes and Single-Family Homes that embody the midcentury modern era's focus on clean lines and a "less is more" philosophy. Enjoy a richer living experience with thoughtfully planned, HOA maintained homes and a variety of amenities in a compact, stylish setting.

The community

Quiet comfort, bold design.

Willow Grove blends open-concept living with natural textures and outdoor gathering spaces. Enjoy wide sidewalks, preserved green spaces, and a boutique collection of floor plans curated for modern families.

Nestled minutes from downtown Waxahachie, the community keeps you close to shopping, dining, and schools without the noise of the city.

At a glance

  • Estimated completion: Spring 2026
  • Single-story + two-story plans
  • Community trails + pocket parks
  • Smart-home ready features
